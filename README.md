# SeafoodSearch
A small Bitsy Game Maker game where you must dive in the sea to find seafood for a restaurant!

There are hungry mouths to feed at the restaurant you work at and you must traverse the sea to collect seafood for your boss. Enjoy the calming waters and marine life along the way! 
Movement controls are WASD, walk into white sprites to interact, press ENTER to continue dialouge. Have fun and thank you so much for playing my game!
